Precision Engineering Meets Personal Service

At GAF, we don't just install aluminium and frameless glass—we craft solutions that transform spaces. Since 2013, we've built our reputation on a simple philosophy: every project deserves owner-level attention and every client deserves honest, reliable service.

  • Owner-Supervised Quality - Direct involvement from consultation to completion ensures your project meets our exacting standards.
  • Proven Expertise - Over a decade of hands-on experience in residential and commercial installations across Johannesburg.
  • Relationship-Focused Approach - We measure success by repeat clients and referrals, not just completed projects.

Our commitment extends beyond exceptional craftsmanship to include reliable timelines and transparent communication. Whether you're enhancing your home with frameless shower enclosures or upgrading your business with stunning storefronts, GAF delivers precision engineering with the personal touch that sets us apart from larger, impersonal competitors.

Premium Materials Only

Our delivery commitments aren't estimates—they're promises backed by meticulous project planning and proven execution.

Custom Solutions

Comprehensive Service
